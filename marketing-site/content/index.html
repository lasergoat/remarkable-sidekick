Remarkable Sidekick

Join the Journey!

One tool for the Remarkable 2

Lockscreen and Template Management

Changing the lockscreen and adding templates requires users to SSH into their devices. Remarkable Sidekick will do this in one click.

User Driven Gallery

Remarkable Sidekick will provide a centralized gallery for users to upload and find lockscreens and templates.

Highlight Extraction

Extract highlighted sections of your books and PDFs to a centralized place like Notion or OneNote.

Daily Newsletter

Assign your Remarkable 2 an email address and have all of your newsletters compiled into a daily PDF that can be read evey morning.
